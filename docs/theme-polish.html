The Polish design language

  • Cards: bordered and softly lifted — a 1px border hairline plus a low, green-slate-tinted shadow.card. Refined, not flat; calm, not floaty.
  • Buttons: a single canonical shape — 52pt tall, 14px radius. The primary CTA carries a faint top-to-bottom green gradient and a soft brand-tinted shadow for gentle depth; the ghost variant is a white surface with a primaryBorder outline.
  • Trust banner: a barely-there green wash (gradients.trust) inside a primaryBorder frame, anchored by a shield-check — the emotional center of the money screen.
  • Density: generous 16–24px rhythm; one primary CTA per screen; every tap target ≥44pt.
  • Counterparties: buyer emerald #38a06b lifts clearly off the deep primary green; seller stays trust-blue #3b6fe0 — three distinguishable identities.

Grays, resolved

  • Six drifting grays collapse into one cool-slate ramp: surface → bg → surfaceAlt → border.
  • Secondary text darkened to clear WCAG 4.5:1; muted text lifted for legibility.
